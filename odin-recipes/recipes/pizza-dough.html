Pizza Dough Recipe

Description

The requirements for this quick and easy recipe are simple. You will need a food processor and only a couple common ingredients. The recipe yields enough dough for two 12" pizzas.

Ingredients

  • 12.5 ounces of all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet of rapid rise yeast
  • 2 Teaspoons of sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 3/4 Cup + 2 tablespoons of water
  • 2 Tablespoons of olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix all of the dry indredients until fully integrated. This takes about 30 seconds in the food processor.
  2. With the processor running, slowly pour both of the liquid infredients in.
  3. Watch closely as the dough mixes in the food processor. Remove from the food processor as the dough begins to form a ball.
  4. Remove from the processor and knead for about a minute on an oiled surface.
  5. Form the kneaded dough into a ball and place in an oiled covered bowl.
  6. Let the dough rise for two hours.
