Chicken Soup Recipe

Description

Here is a delicous and simple chicken soup recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken thighs and 1 chicken breast
  • 2 Diced celery stalks
  • 2 Diced carrots
  • 1/2 White onion
  • 1 container of chicken broth
  • 2 Tablespoons of olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken
  2. Debone and shread the chicken
  3. Lightly sautee onions, celery, and carrots together.
  4. Through the ingredients in a pot and boil for like 20 minutes?
