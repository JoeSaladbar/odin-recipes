Pancakes

Description

Start your morning right with these delicious flapjacks. Here is the easy and quick recipe.

Ingredients

  • 12.5 ounces of all-purpose flour
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 Teaspoons of sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 4 Tablespoons of butter
  • 2 Tablespoons of olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix all of the dry indredients until fully integrated. This takes about 30 seconds in the food processor.
  2. With the processor running, slowly pour both of the liquid infredients in.
  3. Fry them up, takes about 2 minutes on each side.
